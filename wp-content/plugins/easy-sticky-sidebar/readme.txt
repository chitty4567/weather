== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `easy-sticky-sidebar` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or search for "WordPress CTA Plugin" on WordPress.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. You will find 'WP CTA' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
4. Once you are on the WP CTA admin panel, you can edit and change the image, text, fonts, colors and url of the CTA then click save.
5. Choose development mode first so you can test and edit your CTA first. Development mode will only show to admins who are logged in. Live will show to everyone.
5. Go to your home page and refresh. Once the page reloads, you will CTA on the right hand side.
7. When you scroll down the CTA will collapse so that it does not take up too much room.
